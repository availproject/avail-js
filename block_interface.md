## Vocabulary & Structure

### Extrinsic Signature & Call

```
                MultiAddress (Enum)
------------------------------------------------
| Variant      | Type                          |
------------------------------------------------
| 0: Id        | AccountId (32 Bytes)          |
------------------------------------------------
| 1: Index     | u32 (compact)                 |
------------------------------------------------
| 2: Raw       | Vec<u8>                       |
------------------------------------------------
| 3: Address32 | [u8; 32]                      |
------------------------------------------------
| 4: Address20 | [u8; 20]                      |
------------------------------------------------

                MultiSignature (Enum)
------------------------------------------------
| Variant      | Type                          |
------------------------------------------------
| 0: Ed25519   | [u8; 64]                      |
------------------------------------------------
| 1: Sr25519   | [u8; 64]                      |
------------------------------------------------
| 2: Ecdsa     | [u8; 65]                      |
------------------------------------------------

               SignedExtra (Struct)
------------------------------------------------
| Field       | Type                           |
------------------------------------------------
| era         | Era (enum)                     |
------------------------------------------------
| nonce       | u32 (compact)                  |
------------------------------------------------
| tip         | u128 (compact)                 |
------------------------------------------------
| app id      | u32 (compact)                  |
------------------------------------------------


               Extrinsic Signature (Struct)
------------------------------------------------
| Field       | Type                           |
------------------------------------------------
| signer      | MultiAddress                   |
------------------------------------------------
| signature   | MultiSignature                 |
------------------------------------------------
| extra       | SignedExtra                    |
------------------------------------------------

               Extrinsic Call (Struct)
------------------------------------------------
| Field       | Type                           |
------------------------------------------------
| pallet Id   | u8                             |
------------------------------------------------
| variant Id  | u8                             |
------------------------------------------------
| call data   | Vec<u8> or String              |
------------------------------------------------
```

### Extrinsics

```
                                     Signed Extrinsic
+-------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------+
|               Extrinsic Signature               |           Extrinsic Call           |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+-----------+------------+-----------+
|   MultiAddress | MultiSignature | SignedExtra   | Pallet Id | Variant Id | Call Data |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+-----------+------------+-----------+



                                       Extrinsic
+-------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------+
|          [Optional] Extrinsic Signature         |           Extrinsic Call           |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+-----------+------------+-----------+
|   MultiAddress | MultiSignature | SignedExtra   | Pallet Id | Variant Id | Call Data |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+-----------+------------+-----------+



                                      Raw Extrinsic
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|                                   Vec<u8> or String                                  |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+



                               Raw Extrinsic (partially decoded)
+-------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------+
|          [Optional] Extrinsic Signature         |           Extrinsic Call           |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+
|   MultiAddress | MultiSignature | SignedExtra   |          Vec<u8> or String         |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+



                                 Decoded Signed Extrinsic
+-------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------+
|               Extrinsic Signature               |           Extrinsic Call           |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+
|   MultiAddress | MultiSignature | SignedExtra   |                  T                 |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+



                                     Decoded Extrinsic
+-------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------+
|         [Optional] Extrinsic Signature          |           Extrinsic Call           |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+
|   MultiAddress | MultiSignature | SignedExtra   |                  T                 |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+
```

### Block Extrinsics

```
               Metadata (Struct)
------------------------------------------------
| Field       | Type                           |
------------------------------------------------
| tx hash     | u8                             |
------------------------------------------------
| tx index    | u8                             |
------------------------------------------------
| pallet id   | u8                             |
------------------------------------------------
| variant id  | u8                             |
------------------------------------------------
| block id    | u32 or string or H256          |
------------------------------------------------



                                                                             Block Signed Extrinsic
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|                               Decoded Signed Extrinsic                               |                                         Extra Information                                        |
+-------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------+
|               Extrinsic Signature               |           Extrinsic Call           |                        Metadata                        |                Shortcut                 |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+---------+----------+-----------+------------+----------+--------+-------+------------------------+
|   MultiAddress | MultiSignature | SignedExtra   |                  T                 | Tx Hash | Tx Index | Pallet Id | Variant Id | Block Id | App Id | Nonce | [Optional] SS58Address |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+---------+----------+-----------+------------+----------+--------+-------+------------------------+



                                                                                Block Extrinsic
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|                               Decoded Signed Extrinsic                               |                                Extra Information                                |
+-------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------+------------------------+
|         [Optional] Extrinsic Signature          |           Extrinsic Call           |                        Metadata                        |        Shortcut        |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+---------+----------+-----------+------------+----------+------------------------+
|   MultiAddress | MultiSignature | SignedExtra   |                  T                 | Tx Hash | Tx Index | Pallet Id | Variant Id | Block Id | [Optional] SS58Address |
+----------------+----------------+---------------+------------------------------------+---------+----------+-----------+------------+----------+------------------------+



                                                                                Block Raw Extrinsic
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
|                                                                                      |                                              Extra Information                                               |
|                  [Optional] Raw Extrinsic or Raw Extrinsic Call                      +--------------------------------------------------------+-----------------------------------------------------+
|                                                                                      |                        Metadata                        |               [Optional] Signer Payload             |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+---------+----------+-----------+------------+----------+--------+-------+------------------------+-----------+
|                              Vec<u8> or String                                       | Tx Hash | Tx Index | Pallet Id | Variant Id | Block Id | App Id | Nonce | [Optional] SS58Address | Mortality |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+---------+----------+-----------+------------+----------+--------+-------+------------------------+-----------+
```
